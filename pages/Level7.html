Boxes are no longer clickable.

Instead, you'll be judged based on how strong the board's coloring pattern is.

To progress, you'll have to change how your 'Hits' and 'Misses' are decided.

Higher standards will increase how accurate a guess has to be to be counted as a Hit, giving you a stronger board, but reducing how many hits you get.

Lower-standard guesses can also get pretty strong if you spam them long enough, though, as long as it's not too low.

Try to find the Standards that get you to the end the fastest!
